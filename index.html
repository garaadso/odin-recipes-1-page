Quick Pumpkin Spice Latte

This is a great recipe for the holidays; it tastes great and it feels like fall when you drink it.
It is similar to the Starbucks® Pumpkin Spice Latte, but I personally like this better. Top
with whipped cream and pumpkin pie spice.

Pumpkin Spice Latte recipe Details

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk, divided
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup brewed espresso

    Directions

  1. Step 1

    Whisk 1/2 cup milk, sugar, pumpkin puree, pumpkin pie spice,
    and vanilla extract in a small saucepan over low heat. Simmer
    for 5 minutes. Whisk in remaining 1/2 cup milk.

  2. Step 2

    Pour milk mixture through a fine-mesh sieve to remove pulp.
    Return milk mixture to saucepan and simmer, whisking,
    2 minutes. Add espresso and whisk until foamy, 1 minute.
